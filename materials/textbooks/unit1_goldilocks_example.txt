Unit 1 Goldilocks and the three bears - Story time
译林版小学英语五年级上册

【Story Text】
Goldilocks is in the forest. 
She is hungry and thirsty.
She sees a house.

Goldilocks is in the house now.
There is some soup on the table.
This soup is too hot!
This soup is too cold!
This soup is just right!

Goldilocks is tired now.
There are three beds in the room.
This bed is too hard!
This bed is too soft!
This bed is just right!

Goldilocks is afraid!
There are three bears in front of her!
Help! Help!

【Key Vocabulary】
- house (n.) 房子
- room (n.) 房间
- forest (n.) 森林
- soup (n.) 汤
- bear (n.) 熊
- hungry (adj.) 饥饿的
- thirsty (adj.) 口渴的
- tired (adj.) 累的
- afraid (adj.) 害怕的
- hard (adj.) 硬的
- soft (adj.) 软的
- hot (adj.) 热的
- cold (adj.) 冷的

【Key Phrases】
- in the forest 在森林里
- in front of 在...前面
- just right 正合适

【Key Sentences & Grammar】
There is + 单数名词
- There is some soup on the table.
- There is a house in the forest.

There are + 复数名词
- There are three beds in the room.
- There are three bears in front of her.

【Teaching Objectives】
1. Students can understand the story of Goldilocks
2. Students can use "There is/are" to describe things
3. Students can act out the story with emotions
4. Students can talk about their feelings (hungry, thirsty, tired, etc.)

【Teaching Key Points】
1. Understanding the story plot
2. Using "There is/are" correctly
3. Vocabulary about feelings and descriptions

【Teaching Difficulties】
1. The difference between "There is" and "There are"
2. Acting out the story naturally
3. Understanding English story-telling patterns

【Cultural Background】
Goldilocks and the Three Bears is a classic British fairy tale.
It teaches children about respecting others' property and personal space.
